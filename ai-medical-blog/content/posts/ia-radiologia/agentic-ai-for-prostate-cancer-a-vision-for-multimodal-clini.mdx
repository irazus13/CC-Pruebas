## Resumen

While artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly advancing in prostate cancer diagnostics and therapy planning, its impact on everyday clinical decision-making remains marginal. To fill this gap, "Agentic AI systems" are under development: working at an orchestration layer integrating the different AI agents, they are proposed as a powerful tool to synthesize the different clinical information, supporting clinicians' decision-making more effectively.
