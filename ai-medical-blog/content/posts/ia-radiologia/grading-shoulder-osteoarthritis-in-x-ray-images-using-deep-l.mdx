## Resumen

This study attempts to develop a deep learning (DL) model to grade Glenohumeral OA (GHOA) in shoulder x-ray images based on the Hamada classification system. Using a novel image enhancing technique, the final model achieved AUROC scores ranging from 0.87 to 0.96.
