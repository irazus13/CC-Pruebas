## Resumen

Oral cancer represents a significant global health challenge, with rising incidence rates and a dire need for innovative diagnostic and preventive strategies. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into the field of oncology has shown promise in enhancing the early detection and prevention of oral cancer. This systematic review and meta-analysis synthesize current literature on the effectiveness of AI technologies in identifying oral malignancies, focusing on diagnostic accuracy, screening methodologies, and preventive measures.
